About Us

This is a personalized Ecourt web application which provies its services and is dedicated towards a single court. It provides complete information about the cases registered in it... And this application receives updates on every day basis by keeping the track of proceedings of the court which will give an accurate information to the users. This also updates the current status of the cases that are recorded in the application such as-Registered, Trails etc...

User Manual

-> The users can be advocates or clients who can register and then login to search for their required case through the registerd Case Number for the case(CNR).
-> They are provided with the feasibility of saving their cases to "My Cases" section and are free to discard them from the section whenever they want.
-> The users can update their passwords by entering the old password and setting up a new password.
-> They can serach for the case details without logging in but to save the case they must login with their enrollment id and password which are provided by them at the time of registration.
